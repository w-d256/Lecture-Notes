        This is the lecture note for the graduate course \textit{Functional Analysis} taught by Prof. Yutian Li in 2023 Fall at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. The note is edited and revised with the support of ChatGPT. The materials presume a basic knowledge of functional analysis, so contents that are close to the boundary of these prerequisites may be omitted. Topics of the note include: review of linear functional analysis, weak convergence, spectral theory, semigroups, Sobolev spaces, and calculus of variations.
